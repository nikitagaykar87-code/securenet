How to Report - Step by Step Guide

1

Visit the Portal

Go to cybercrime.gov.in and click on 'Report Cyber Crime'.

2

Fill the Form

Provide incident details, date, time, and a full description.

3

Attach Evidence

Upload screenshots, transaction details, and logs.

4

Submit & Track

Receive acknowledgment number to track status.

Go to Cybercrime Portal

What Happens After You Report?

  • • Acknowledgment: Complaint number generated
  • • Review: Cyber cell review within 48–72 hours
  • • Contact: Officer may contact you
  • • Evidence: Digital forensic analysis
  • • Freeze: Bank accounts may be frozen
  • • Investigation: Legal action begins
