Emergency Action Protocol

Actions to take within the Golden Hour

01. Block & Freeze

Immediately notify your bank to block all debit/credit cards and UPI services to prevent further unauthorized transfers.

02. Preserve Evidence

Capture screenshots of the scam messages, transaction IDs, and communication logs for reporting.

03. Dial 1930

Call the National Cybercrime Helpline 1930 immediately to report financial fraud in progress.
