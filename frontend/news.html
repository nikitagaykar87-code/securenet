🕵️‍♀️ Real Cyber Crime Case Studies & Prevention Tips

Case Study 1: Digital Arrest Video Call Scam

Fake police officers forced a victim via WhatsApp video call to transfer money by threatening arrest.

Read Full Report
✓ Police never arrest through video call.
✓ No authority asks for money online.
✓ Disconnect immediately if threatened.
✓ Call 1930 & local police.

Case Study 2: Fake Job Offer Email Scam

Victims paid fake “registration fees” after receiving bogus HR calls and emails.

Read Full Report
✓ No genuine company asks for money.
✓ Verify official websites & HR emails.
✓ Ignore WhatsApp/Telegram job offers.
✓ Report immediately to cybercrime.gov.in.

Case Study 3: Matrimony Fake Profile Scam

Scammers used fake profiles on social media and matrimonial sites to emotionally manipulate victims.

Read Full Report
✓ Verify identity before trusting.
✓ Never send money to online matches.
✓ Report fake accounts immediately.
✓ Avoid sharing personal photos.

Case Study 4: Fake Trading App Investment Scam

A ₹24 crore scam used fake investment apps and WhatsApp groups promising guaranteed profits.

Read Full Report
✓ Avoid Telegram/WhatsApp investment groups.
✓ Check SEBI registration before investing.
✓ No platform can assure guaranteed profit.
✓ Use only official, trusted apps.

✔️ Essential Cyber Safety Checklist

  • ✔ Always verify the URL before logging in.
  • ✔ Enable 2-factor authentication everywhere.
  • ✔ Use different passwords for different services.
  • ✔ Regularly update apps to avoid vulnerabilities.
  • ✔ Never share banking details with anyone.
